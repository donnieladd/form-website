WHAT IS MAKING IT FEEL “GAME-LIKE”
These are the things currently pushing it toward “video game”:
1. Too many sharp reactive glows
Everything reacts aggressively.
Instead:
slower fade
softer bloom
delayed movement
atmospheric response
Think:
 “breathing.”
 Not:
 “triggering.”

2. Too much visible grid structure
The visible topology/grid systems feel tactical.
Reduce:
visible grid density
sharp wireframes
HUD overlays
hard corner framing
Increase:
atmospheric gradients
depth fog
cinematic light
environmental movement

3. Motion is too direct
Current motion:
follows cursor too tightly
feels engineered
feels interactive-first
Instead:
 motion should feel:
environmental
magnetic
orbital
drifting
gravitational
Less:
 mouse tracking.
More:
 space responding.

4. Particle scale
The particles are too large.
You already caught this.
The solution:
nanite scale
tiny intelligence particles
micro atmospheric movement
subtle field reactions
The smaller they become,
 the more expensive the UI feels.

 Reduce scale.
